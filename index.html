About Us

Do you know who we are ?

We at ATITHIADVISOR are providing a platform to share this other side of thestory.ATITHIADVISOR is primarily for all Stay Facility Providers in hospitality industrylike Hotels, Homestays, Campsites, Bagpackers’ Stays and unique experiencestays such as Treehouses, Igloos, Houseboats etc. Facility Providers will nowhave opportunity to share their experience with the Atithi that have stayed withthem and used their facilities on a common platform. Registered Users ofATITHIADVISOR can even search feedback of a guest shared by other RegisteredFacility Provider. Getting these insights will help the Facility Provider to “knowthe Customer” beforehand and this preparedness will assist in better service tothe Guest
