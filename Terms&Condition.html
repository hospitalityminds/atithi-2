We are immensely proud of the community are building built which helps both hospitality fraternity, even guests and travel community .

We believe every experience counts and every small effort builds a strong community. Not just the ones who have made reservation but all who have checked in along, all of them should be reviewed / rated. That's what Atithiadvisor.com (2M Softwares) stands for.

It might be easier to give in to censorship and remove reviews that a guest / business owner disagrees with.
